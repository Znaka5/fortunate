🃏 The Cards Whisper

🪙 1 Card Drawn – The Mirror Test

The card defines you. No past. No future. Just a single, razor-edged truth.
It stares back harder than you expect.

  • A single dominant force has claimed your life.
  • You will dream of it tonight.
  • No matter what it was, it now walks parallel to you.
  • Mirrors, shadows, and screens may flicker—just once. Enough to make you doubt.
  • Do not speak the name of the card aloud after midnight.
  • If it smiles in your dream, do not smile back.

🃙 2 Cards Drawn – The Stranger’s Choice

One card is real. The other lies.
But lies can still come true if believed hard enough.

  • Dual forces begin circling you. You won’t know which one is acting.
  • Choices become blurred: your yeses feel like maybes, your no’s come with whispers.
  • The false one may offer you exactly what you want.
  • The real one will wait at the edge of your reflection—where fog shouldn’t form.
  • Flip a coin on decisions for the next 24 hours.
  • If the coin lands on its edge, leave the room.

🃙 3 Cards Drawn – The Spiral Begins

Past. Present. What follows you.
But the third card doesn’t walk in time. It just waits.

  • The first card is what haunts you.
  • The second is what holds you here.
  • The third is what will find you eventually—when you are alone.
  • Lights may dim in threes. Laughter may echo without a mouth.
  • Speak only in odd numbers when you feel watched.
  • Never knock three times unless you know who knocks back.

🃙 4 Cards Drawn – The Circle Closes

North. East. South. West.
You're surrounded. This isn’t a reading. It’s a summoning.

  • You’ve completed the deck’s ritual unknowingly.
  • Protection or possession—the difference is just perspective now.
  • You may feel safe. That’s not always good.
  • The air around you knows your name. Sometimes it says it back.
  • Salt will fail.
  • The fourth card always lies. Find out which. Do not trust the others either.

🃏 The Cards Whisper — And Smile

🪞 Mirror

You saw yourself.
You think it was you.
It waved first.

— When you leave the room, it stays watching.

🩸 Blood

It smelled sweet.
Like memory.
Like family.

— It spelled your name in droplets on the ceiling. You weren’t bleeding.

⚔️ Broken Sword

It’s useless now. Or sacred.
Some things can only be broken once.
And only for you.

— It hums when you're angry. You don't know why.

🍀 Luck

You won something.
But no one remembers what.
And the air's just a little… thinner now.

— You keep surviving. Even when you shouldn’t. They’re watching.

🕳️ Spades

Something was buried.
Then it started knocking.

— But maybe it just wants to be found. Maybe it’s you.

🎲 Fortune

The wheel spun.
You closed your eyes.
You woke up in the same place—but something’s missing.

— You can’t lose what was never yours, right?

🌟 Star

It blinked.
You felt loved.
Then it screamed.

— You still see it sometimes when you close your eyes, smiling too wide.

⛧ Pentagram

Drawn in chalk. Or bone. Or was that just dust?
Either way, it worked.

— Now the room feels safe. Too safe. Like something's protecting you. Or feeding.

👁️ You

All the cards were about you anyway.
Weren’t they?

— Something inside you knows what’s coming. And it’s smiling.

👑 Queen of 10’s

She offered you a choice.
You said yes.
You don’t remember the question.

— She’s counting down. You’re always on the tenth second.
